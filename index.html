Game Rules

  • In your turn -roll thedice (at least once ) and accumlate the result in "current`
  • You can roll again or click "Hold" to save the points from "Current" and end the turn`
  • Note! if you get 6-6 you will lose all your points from "Current" and the turn will go to opponent`
  • If you managed reach exactly the target score - you win if you passed it -you lose ..
